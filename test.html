Here are some chemical formulas related to metal oxides:\n\n* **General formation of metal oxide:**\n Metal + Oxygen → Metal oxide\n\n* **Formation of Copper(II) oxide:**\n 2Cu + O₂ → 2CuO\n\n* **Formation of Aluminium oxide:**\n 4Al + 3O₂ → 2Al₂O₃\n\n* **Aluminium oxide reacting with hydrochloric acid (acidic behavior):**\n Al₂O₃ + 6HCl → 2AlCl₃ + 3H₂O\n\n* **Aluminium oxide reacting with sodium hydroxide (basic behavior):**\n Al₂O₃ + 2NaOH → 2NaAlO₂ + H₂O\n\n* **Reduction of Zinc oxide with carbon:**\n ZnO(s) + C(s) → Zn(s) + CO(g)
